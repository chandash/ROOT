Are you getting the login screen repeatedly after giving the username and password correctly in Cygwin? ( Profile problem ).

How to solve this?

1.) Login through telnet to any of the machine with your ID.
2.) Execute the following commands from your home directory (Since below files are resided inside your home directory).
  1. mv .profile .profile_old
  2. mv .dt .dt_old
  3. mv .dtprofile .dtprofile_old
  4. mv .Xauthority .Xauthority_old
  5. mv .Xdefaults .Xdefaults_old
  6. mv .TTauthority .TTauthority_old
  7. mv .user .user_old
 3.) Creating new profile by executing stdenv by
$ /ap/local/5.8/shell/stdenv
